Rokutal-Wiki

Rokutal is a offline text encoder-decoder which can function as password manager or a vault to save other sensetive data.

Rokku (ロック) and tAla (ताल) both mean lock in Japanese and Sanskrit resp. so Rokutal means locklock I guess.
